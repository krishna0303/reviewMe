Earphones are just an electrical device that converts audio signals into sound waves it's employed by listeners to listen to the radio, MP3 players, etc. it’s sort of a small speaker worn in ears or around your ears, alittle mouthpiece is attached to the earphone to receive calls and to reply . SoundCore life note:

Soundcore life note is a product of Anker company founded in the year 2011 in September. It’s based in Shenzhen, Guangdong, a Chinese electronics brand that is owned by Anker innovations.

Image Courtesy: theqna

Soundcore life note is a true wireless Bluetooth headset available in both black and white colors. It has accurate, immersive, and ultra-realistic audio. It calls quality noise reduction technology and four microphones on each earbud that help users receive calls or listen to their favorite music even in public places without disturbance. It works for 40 hours with just a single recharge when listening to music. You can charge it just for 10 minutes and then enjoy your music for up to one hour without interrupted connectivity with Bluetooth 5.0. The Soundcore life note is waterproof; it can withstand sweat, rain, and water too. Soundcore life note is a great product and is a must-buy product that comes under 3000. All the above features have made us include Soundcore Life note in our list of best wireless earphones under 3000.

Noise shots groove:

Noise shots groove are the wireless earphones by the company Gonoise founded by Amit Khatri and Gaurav Khatri. Noise shots groove is a truly wireless earphone set available in three colors like matte black, sage gray, and midnight blue.

Image Courtesy: theqna

The device has a very stable connection, making it easy for users to connect it with their devices with the dual-channel transmission feature. The device also has powerful bass with a 6mm driver speaker. The noise shots groove is well equipped with touch controls to help users use those touch controls to easily change the songs, adjust volume, and receive or end calls. It has 15 hours of playback time with just a single charge, so you can listen to your favorite songs nonstop for 15 long hours while traveling. It uses a Type C cable to charge, so you can also charge it with your smartphone charger available. The device is waterproof and can be used near the pool; it is very lightweight and can be carried anywhere easily. The device also has an inbuilt microphone, making the voice clearly without any lag so that you can use it easily without any trouble. All the above features have made us include Noise Shots Groove in our list of best wireless earphones under 3000
